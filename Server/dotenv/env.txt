DB: Connect to MongoDb url (Compass or Atlas)
JWTPRIVATEKEY: Give a defined keyword for your token.
SALT: Give a random number (examples:10,20)
PORT: Port to connect to backend. 
JWTPRIVATEKEYADMIN: Give a defined keyword for your Admin side token.
CLOUDINARY_CLOUD_NAME : Login to Cloudinary and paste the name. 
CLOUDINARY_API_KEY : Login to Cloudinary and paste the API_KEY.
CLOUDINARY_API_SECRET : Login to Cloudinary and paste the Secret key.
EMAIL: Provide the email which you want to send the otp.
PASSWORD: Generate the password from google account.
